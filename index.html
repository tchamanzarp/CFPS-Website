Why CFPS

Zero barriers. Real reps.

CFPS is for any UH student who wants a career in financial planning, wealth management, or finance. Here is the deal, up front.

Open door

No interviews, no application

Everyone is welcome. Fill out the interest form and you are in.

Dues, stated plainly

$40 per semester

Or $70 for the full year paid upfront, a $10 savings. Every dollar goes back into the club.

All majors

Not just the PFP track

Open to ambitious students in any major who are interested in financial planning and wealth management careers.

Weekly rhythm

Thursdays, every week

Chapter meetings run every Thursday. That is the main weekly touchpoint all semester.

What you get

Built to make you hireable

Everything CFPS runs is aligned with what recruiters actually look for.

Access

Real industry reps

Networking with industry professionals and alumni, guest speakers, corporate office visits, alumni panels, and a mock interview day.

Skills

Recruiter ready training

Hands on technical workshops, case competitions, and direct exposure to eMoney, MoneyGuidePro, and Holistiplan.

Support

Money where it matters

Funding support for the SIE, Series exams, and CFP Exam Review, plus scholarships and graduation cords for active members.
