About CFPS

Bridging campus and the industry

Who we are, what we run, and why this club exists.

Our mission
“The mission of the Cougar Financial Planning Society is to develop industry ready professionals equipped to achieve lasting success within the wealth management and planning sector.”
Mission statement
What we do

We bridge academics and modern advisory practice by converting collegiate ambition into validated, technical, corporate ready skill. That happens through structured professional development, hands on case competitions, and industry vetted technical training.

Certifications, technical workshops, and case modeling are aligned directly with recruiter expectations, so the work you put in here shows up in interviews.

Why we exist: breaking into wealth management can feel gated behind experience you do not have yet. CFPS levels that. We are a student led organization open to every major, with no interviews and no application, that puts real training, real firms, and real reps in front of you before you graduate.
