Dues

Dues for 2026 to 2027

Starting this year, CFPS membership requires dues. Here is exactly what they cost and where the money goes.

Per semester

$40

Pay as you go, semester by semester.

Full year

$70 paid upfront

A $10 savings compared to paying semester by semester. Paying in full is optional, not required. It is simply there for members who know they are in it for the year and want the better rate.

Dues go directly back into the organization: funding professional certification support (SIE, Series exams, CFP Exam Review), case competition prizes, socials and networking nights, and scholarships for active members.
